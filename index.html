Privacy Policy for خماسيات (Quran Fives)

This Privacy Policy applies to خماسيات (Quran Fives), developed by Shaaib Saleh (shaaib7sky@gmail.com), available on Google Play as com.shoaib.quranfives.

English Version

Scope: This policy applies to the Quran Fives memorization assistant app, which helps users master verse numbers, page starts, quizzes, repetition counters, "fives" audio, navigation techniques, and grouped verse lists.

Information Collection: No personal information is collected, stored, or processed—no names, emails, device identifiers, analytics, or location data.

Usage Behavior: All memorization sessions, quizzes, counters, and audio playback run entirely on the device without logging or external sharing.

Third Parties: Because no data exists, nothing is shared with third parties; there are no analytics, ads, or external tracking integrations.

Security: Even though no personal data is stored, we apply secure development practices, updates, and patches to keep the app stable and safe.

Changes to Policy: Any updates will be posted inside the app with the effective date (e.g., "Effective Date: March 29, 2026").

Contact: For questions, email shaaib7sky@gmail.com with "Privacy Policy" in the subject line.
